STACK SCHOLAR

Our product is an open sourced peer reviewed journal website that caters to students and graduate professionals in the process of publishing research. We are creating an online repository to store, update, comment, and contribute to fellow scholarly journals that have yet to be published. Our goal is to facilitate open dialogue between individuals and groups that may have never had the opportunity to collaborate before our website was created.

We are a non profit entity that supports all scholarly works.

GETTING STARTED

By creating an account you will be able to upload documents that you can then share and recieve comments and suggestions to help you complete your work.

We believe people can offer insight and help when working in a collaborative model. We are constantly updating and reviewing our site and appreciate any feedback in an effort to create a useful and practical experience.
